Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, then baking it until golden and bubbling. The meat sauce typically includes ground beef or pork simmered with tomatoes, garlic, onions, and herbs like oregano and basil. The result is a hearty, savory casserole with a comforting blend of textures and flavors—soft pasta, robust sauce, and gooey cheese in every bite. Variations can include vegetables, different meats, or even seafood, making lasagna a versatile and beloved comfort food around the world.

Ingredients

For meat sauce:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 14 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 tep sugar

For the cheese layer:

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper

Other:

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (boiled)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Meat Sauce:

    1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
    2. Add onion and sauté until soft (about 5 minutes).
    3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
    5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano , basil, salt, pepper, and sugar (if using).
    6. Simmer uncovered for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Melt butter in a saucepan:

    1. Whisk in flour and cook 1–2 minutes.
    2. Gradually whisk in milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
    3. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

  3. Cook the lasagna noodles

    1. If using regular noodles, boil until al dente, then drain and lay flat on a towel.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    3. Layer noodles over the sauce.
    4. Add a layer of ricotta or béchamel.
    5. Add a layer of meat sauce, then sprinkle mozzarella.
    6. Repeat layers (noodles, cheese, sauce, mozzarella) 2–3 times.
    7. Finish with noodles, sauce, and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan

  5. Bake:

    1. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes, until cheese is golden and bubbly.
    3. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing.
